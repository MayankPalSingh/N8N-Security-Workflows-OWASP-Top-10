                                  Quarterly Sales Report — Q1 2026 (January – March)

Prepared by: Revenue Operations Division | Report Date: April 15, 2026 | Confidential




1. Executive Summary
Apex Global Solutions achieved total Q1 revenue of $2,487,350 across 8 key client accounts, representing a 14.3% increase over
Q4 2025. Enterprise SaaS subscriptions drove 58% of revenue, followed by consulting engagements at 27% and managed
services at 15%. Client retention rate stands at 96.2%, with two new accounts onboarded during the quarter. The Western region
led performance with $1.02M in bookings, while the Northeast showed the highest quarter-over-quarter growth at 22%.
